About Automoblox

Automoblox are precision-crafted wooden car kits that blend minimal design with a premium tactile building experience. This redesign project highlights five signature models with a focus on clean presentation, strong typography, and a responsive layout system.

A Modern Take on a Classic Toy

Automoblox merges traditional wooden craftsmanship with interchangeable high-precision components, encouraging creativity, exploration, and hands-on learning. Each car is made from premium beechwood, paired with bold polycarbonate details that bring personality and modern design flair.

This microsite redesign focuses on simplicity, brand consistency, and the unique identity of each model through color-coded visuals and refined interface styling.
